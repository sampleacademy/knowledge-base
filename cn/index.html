知识库

欢迎来到Sample Academy知识库！

这里我们提供一些信息和指南，可以帮助您使用我们的网站和服务。如果您仍有疑问，请与我们联系.

访问Sample Academy

您可以通过以下方式之一访问我们的网站。

  1. 在您的浏览器中输入https://www.sampleacademy.org/。
  2. 在如Google、Bing、DuckDuckGo、Yandex等搜索引擎中输入 Sample Academy。

我们强烈建议您收藏我们的网站。

注册

为了访问我们所有的网站和服务，您必须注册一个Sample Academy / SamplEdu中央认证服务（CAS）账户。我们在这里提供指导，教您如何注册一个Sample Academy / SamplEdu中央认证服务（CAS）账户。请注意，您可以使用您的微软账户的电子邮箱或者非微软账户的电子邮箱来注册。

  1. 用微软账户的电子邮箱注册
    2.

    步骤 1: 访问https://www.sampleacademy.org/cas/或在Sample Academy网站菜单处点击 👤。

    步骤 2: 点击登录 & 注册按钮。

    步骤 3: 点击创建一个!。

    步骤 4: 选择使用 Microsoft 帐户注册。

    步骤 5: 在框中输入您的 Microsoft 账户的电子邮箱然后点击下一步。

    步骤 6: 输入您的 Microsoft 账户的密码然后点击登录。

    步骤 7: 选择您是否想保持登录状态。

    如果您不是正在使用您自己的设备，我们建议您选择否。

    步骤 8: 审查权限，并点击接受。

    如果您不接受，您将无法注册一个Sample Academy / SamplEdu CAS 账户。

    步骤 9: 填写所有与您的基本信息有关的框，并点击 继续。

    步骤 10: 您将自动登录并看到Sample Academy的主页。注册成功!

  2. Register with a non-Microsoft account email
    3.

    Step 1: Visit https://www.sampleacademy.org/cas/ or click on 👤 on the menu of Sample Academy website.

    Step 2: Click on Log In & Sign Up button.

    Step 3: Click on Create one!.

    Step 4: Select Sign up with email.

    Step 5: Type your non-Microsoft account email in the block and click on Next.

    Step 6: Get the verification code from your email mailbox or spam section.

    Step 7: Type the verification code in the block and click on Sign in.

    Step 8: Review the permissions and click on Accept.

    If you do not accept, your will fail to register a Sample Academy / SamplEdu CAS account.

    Step 9: Fill in all the blocks that are related to your basic information and click on Continue.

    Step 10: You will be logged in automatically and see the homepage of Sample Academy. Registration success!

If you use a Microsoft account email to register, you will login to our websites and services with your Microsoft account email and password in the future. However, due to security consideration, if you use a non-Microsoft account email to register, you will have to login to our websites and services with verification code every time in the future.

Login

When you have a Sample Academy / SamplEdu Central Authentication Service account, you can login to all of our websites and services with the account and its credential. Similar to the registration process. There are two methods to login, you can login with either your Microsoft account email or non-Microsoft account email.

  1. Login with a Microsoft account email
    2.

    Step 1: Visit https://www.sampleacademy.org/cas/ or click on 👤 on the menu of Sample Academy website.

    Step 2: Click on Log In & Sign Up button.

    Step 3: Type your Microsoft account email in the block and click on Next.

    Step 4: Enter your Microsoft account password and click on Sign in.

    Step 5: Choose whether you want to stay signed in.

    We recommend you to choose No if you are not using your own device.

    Step 6: You will be logged in and see Sample Academy website or service. Login success!

  2. Login with a non-Microsoft account email
    3.

    Step 1: Visit https://www.sampleacademy.org/cas/ or click on 👤 on the menu of Sample Academy website.

    Step 2: Click on Log In & Sign Up button.

    Step 3: Type your non-Microsoft account email in the block and click on Next.

    Step 4: Get the verification code from your email mailbox or spam section.

    Step 5: Type the verification code in the block and click on Sign in.

    Step 6: You will be logged in and see Sample Academy website or service. Login success!

Due to security consideration, if you use a non-Microsoft account email to register, you have to login to our websites and services with verification code every time in the future. If there are suspicious activities with your account (e.g.: login too many time with verification code in a short time), we will block your account temporarily to protect it. You can contact us to unlock your account.

Logout

In order to be convenient enough, you will not be logged out until you close your web browser and clear the cookies. Simply speaking, if you want to logout, you just need to clear the cookies, close your web browser, and end all the browsing sessions.

To provide the best experiences, we use technologies like cookies to store and/or access device information. Consenting to these technologies will allow us to process data such as browsing behavior or unique IDs on this site. Not consenting or withdrawing consent, may adversely affect certain features and functions. Please see our cookies policy for more information.
