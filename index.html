Knowledge Base

Welcome to the knowledge base of Sample Academy!

Here we provide some information and guide that can help you when using our website and services. If you still have questions, please contact us.

Visit Sample Academy

You can visit our website by doing one of the followings:

  1. Type https://www.sampleacademy.org/ in your web browser.
  2. Type Sample Academy in search engines such as Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Yandex, and so on.

We highly recommend you to bookmark our website.

Registration

In order to access all of our services, you have to register a Sample Academy / SamplEdu Central Authentication Service (CAS) account. We provide instruction here to teach you how to register a Sample Academy / SamplEdu Central Authentication Service (CAS) account. Notice that you can use either your Microsoft account email or non-Microsoft account email to register.

  1. Register with a Microsoft account email
    2.

    Step 1: Visit https://www.sampleacademy.org/cas/ or click on 👤 on the menu of Sample Academy website.

    Step 2: Click on Log In & Sign Up button.

    Step 3: Click on Create one!.

    Step 4: Select Sign up with Microsoft.

    Step 5: Type your Microsoft account email in the block and click on Next.

    Step 6: Enter your Microsoft account password and click on Sign in.

    Step 7: Choose whether you want to stay signed in.

    We recommend you to choose No if you are not using your own device.

    Step 8: Review the permissions and click on Accept.

    If you do not accept, your will fail to register a Sample Academy / SamplEdu CAS account.

    Step 9: Fill in all the blocks that are related to your basic information and click on Continue.

    Step 10: You will be logged in automatically and see the homepage of Sample Academy. Registration success!

  2. Register with a non-Microsoft account email
    3.

    Step 1: Visit https://www.sampleacademy.org/cas/ or click on 👤 on the menu of Sample Academy website.

    Step 2: Click on Log In & Sign Up button.

    Step 3: Click on Create one!.

    Step 4: Select Sign up with email.

    Step 5: Type your email address in the block and click on Next.

    Step 6: Get the verification code from your email mailbox or spam section.

    Step 7: Type the verification code in the block and click on Sign in.

    Step 8: Review the permissions and click on Accept.

    If you do not accept, your will fail to register a Sample Academy / SamplEdu CAS account.

    Step 9: Fill in all the blocks that are related to your basic information and click on Continue.

    Step 10: You will be logged in automatically and see the homepage of Sample Academy. Registration success!

If you use a Microsoft account email to register, you will login to our website and services with your Microsoft account email and password in the future. However, due to security consideration, if you use a non-Microsoft account email to register, you will have to login to our website and service with verification code every time in the future.
